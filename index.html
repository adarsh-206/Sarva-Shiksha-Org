About Us

Sarv Shiksha Organsiation, is an Indian social organization, is directly benefitting over 1025 children and their families every year. The groundwork for a better life lies in education. It is the most powerful catalyst for social transformation. However, a child cannot be educated in isolation. Only an empowered family, especially the mother, would be willing to educate their child. Thus, rather than focusing on only one stage of a person's life, we believe in the lifecycle approach. Education empowers an individual to earn their livelihood and also increases one's awareness on a range of issues. From healthcare, to appropriate social behavior, to understanding one's rights – Sarva Shikha Organisation seeks to educate, empower and cultivate better citizens.

Why Donate Us ?

In today’s fast-paced world, everyone wants to raise their living standards. They are always working hard to give themselves and their loved ones a better life. But in this race of life, they do not get the time for social service.

Inner Satisfaction

Human beings are social people. They take care of each other and feel good when someone extends their help to them in the time of need. So, when they find someone who they care about, in need of help, they gladly help them, without expecting any kind of a favour in return. This gives them a feeling of satisfaction and fulfilment.

Trust Factor

In childhood, we had learned a proverb, “Charity begins at home”. This is a particularly important lesson in everyone’s life. People are more eager to extend their helping hand to a local organization whose work they can easily monitor on a personal level.

Charity begins at home

Face value is an especially important thing in this modern world. People tend to trust people who have a clean image and are publicly acceptable. So, when such a person appeals for help, people tend to open their arms without giving it a second thought. For example, if Shah Rukh Khan endorses an NGO, it is sure to get more help than the NGO he does not endorse.
